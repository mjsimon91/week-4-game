Dunk the Cookies

You will be given a random number at the start of the game

There are four cookies below. By clicking on a cookie, you will be add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the number. Just like blackjack or The Price is Right, you will lose if your score is above the number.

You will not know the value of the cookie until you click on it...and they will change with each game.
